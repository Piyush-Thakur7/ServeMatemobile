🔍 Built for Transparency

What is ServeMate?

A micro-donation platform that bridges the trust gap between givers and doers — with proof at every step.

❌ The Problem Today

❌ You donate and hope for the best
❌ No visibility into fund usage
❌ Random volunteers, no accountability
❌ No feedback loop from NGO to donor
❌ Trust erodes, donations decline

✅ How ServeMate Fixes It

✅ Real-time task tracking
✅ Video + photo + GPS proof
✅ Verified NGOs with own volunteers only
✅ Gamification keeps you engaged
✅ Rate & review every work done
🔄 The Flow
